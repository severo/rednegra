In July of 2025, as the Parquet format was becoming increasingly popular, I created the [Awesome Parquet](https://github.com/severo/awesome-parquet) repository to record the curation I was doing on the topic. Instead of keeping a personal list, I decided to share it with the community, following the example of other ["Awesome" lists](https://github.com/sindresorhus/awesome) on GitHub.

The repository includes a collection of resources about Parquet (tools, libraries, best practices, etc.) I regularly receive contributions from the community, on GitHub or via other channels, and I try to keep the list up to date with the latest developments in the Parquet ecosystem.

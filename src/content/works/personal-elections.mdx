## 2024 Legislative Elections in France

In July of 2024, I took a week off after the legislative elections in France. I had a specific goal. I wanted to replicate the striking animation called ["Shifting Winds of Belgian Politics"](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/karim-douieb_belgium-election2024-datavisualization-activity-7211630615757033473-qfpk) by Karim Douieb.

As I often do, I tackled the technical difficulty one step at a time.

I manually assigned a [left-right position to each political party](https://observablehq.com/@severo/nuances), and looked at the [repartition of votes along that scale](https://observablehq.com/@severo/distribution-gauche-droite-legislatives-2024):

<Figure id="./personal-left-right.png" href="https://observablehq.com/@severo/distribution-gauche-droite-legislatives-2024" />

Then I [replicated the wind effect](https://observablehq.com/@severo/wind) where each particle flows to the right or to the left, with a trail.

After the first round, I first fetched the data for the participation (the vote is not mandatory in France, so the participation is an important metric).

In 2018, I was fascinated by grid cartograms, and I wrote a review about them in an [Observable notebook](https://observablehq.com/@severo/grid-cartograms). It has been one of the most popular among my notebooks.

I then decided to publish a repository on GitHub, named [data-grid-cartograms](https://github.com/severo/data-grid-cartograms), to share grid cartograms in a data format. It contains grid cartograms for France, the US, Bolivia, Italy, Africa and the world.

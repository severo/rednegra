In 2025, a question arose in a Slack channel about the [Foursquare Places dataset](https://huggingface.co/datasets/foursquare/fsq-os-places). The dataset is split into 100 files grouping the places by region. I quickly created a [tool to locate these regions on a map](https://observablehq.com/@severo/hello-foursquare-places). Clicking a dot sends to the data file.

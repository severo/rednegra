In 2018, my first project as a dataviz freelancer was to port a webapp from ActionScript 3 to D3.js. The project was commissioned by [Moritz Stefaner](./references/moritz-stefaner), a well-known data visualization designer and co-host of the [Data Stories podcast](https://datastori.es/). We realized the project in one month, in tandem with [Philippe Rivière](https://illisible.net/philippe-riviere).

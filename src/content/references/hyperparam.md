In 2024, when I announced that I was leaving Hugging Face as a permanent employee and was available for freelance work, I was quickly contacted by [Kenny Daniel](https://www.linkedin.com/in/kennydaniel/). I already had the chance to exchange with him in the past about his starship project [hyparquet](https://github.com/hyparam/hyparquet) while we integrated it in Hugging Face Hub. He was looking for a software engineer to help him build [Hyperparam](https://hyperparam.app), a data visualization and exploration tool for large datasets. I joined the project as a freelance software engineer in late 2024, and I'm still working on it in 2026.

When I announced in 2024 that I was going freelancer again, [Jed Sundwall](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jedsundwall/), which I already exchanged with while at Hugging Face, proposed me to work with him on [Source Cooperative](https://source.coop), a catalogue of open datasets, published in [Cloud-native formats](https://cloudnativegeo.org/). I had the chance to collaborate on this project, mainly incorporating new React viewers for [parquet](https://github.com/source-cooperative/parquet-table/) and [CSV](https://github.com/source-cooperative/csv-table/) files.
